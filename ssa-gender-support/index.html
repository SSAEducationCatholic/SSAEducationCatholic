Support and Services

Courage

Courage is a support ministry for men and women 18 years and up who experience same-sex attraction and want pastoral guidance, fellowship, and help striving for chastity.

Support options: confidentiality is assured in all options.

For Men: Long Island Courage Chapter

For Women: Contact Yvonne at 516-413-1212 by call or text.

For Young Adults (18-30): miguel_apatan23z@yahoo.com

For Teens: see the Courage FAQs page for guidance and chapter information.

EnCourage

EnCourage supports parents, spouses, siblings, and other loved ones seeking Catholic guidance, spiritual support, and practical help responding with compassion.

Contact: Long Island EnCourage Chapter
