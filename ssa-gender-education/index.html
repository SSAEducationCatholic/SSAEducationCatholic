Educational Resources

The authentically and faithfully Catholic websites and educational resources listed below are rich in beauty to educate, form, and transform Catholics seeking clarity on these topics. They are especially helpful for those experiencing same-sex attraction, gender identity discordance, and for clergy, religious, educators, and lay leaders.

Catholic Teaching loves and embraces all persons as beloved sons and daughters of God. Pastoral accompaniment is intended to be loving and profound, respecting the dignity of each person and helping each person flourish in freedom according to God’s loving plan.

Unfortunately, many persons embracing LGBTQ identities have not had the benefit of a complete Catholic understanding or pastoral guidance. These resources help bridge that gap with truth and charity.
